The methodology to check-up Twitter accounts includes :

  • Check the applications authorized to use your Twitter account (here)
  • Check the active sessions (here)
  • Check the history of connections (here) (it gives you information on connections for the past month)
  • Download all data for further investigation (here) (it gives you information on connections for the past two months)
